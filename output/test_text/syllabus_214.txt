Teaching and Research Interests

Comparative/World/Global South literatures and cultures. Caribbean, Cuban, and Puerto Rican
studies with focus on print, visual, and diaspora cultures and expression. New World Baroque and
Neobaroque literary and cultural studies. Transcultural James Joyce studies. Critical, literary,
postcolonial theory. East/New World cultural encounters.
