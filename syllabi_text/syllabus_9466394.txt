Arabic Readings in Islamic Texts
ARA 360L
TTH 12:30PM- 2:00PM MEZ 1.210
This seminar provides an opportunity to directly engage the language of the
Qurʾān and to explore the political, philosophical, and theological writings of
some of the key thinkers of classical and modern lslam. This course will involve
a weekly Qurʾānic memorization and reading assignment. A good knowledge of
Arabic is required to maximize the learning in this course. The discussions in this
course will be conducted in Arabic. The course covers most of the sub-genres of
the field of Islamic studies such as Qurʾān, ḥadīth, tafsīr, fiqh, kalām, falsafa,
taṣawwuf, tarīkh, and siyāsa.
For inquiries about this course contact
Dr. Samy Ayoub
 sayoub@utexas.edu
