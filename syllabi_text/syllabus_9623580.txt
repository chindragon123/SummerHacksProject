Objectives:
1. To increase awareness and knowledge of food, nutrition, public health, and culture within the context
   of the world
2. To experience practice-based education in nutrition in Sicily and how it differs from the United
    States.
3. To learn about nutrition related issues in Sicily – with a focus on the impact of the Mediterranean diet
    on health.
4. To interact and develop relationships with faculty, students, dietitians and health care professionals at
    schools, hospitals, and clinic facilities
5. To experience living in the environment of an Sicilian society and how this affects nutrition, diet and
    health of humans.
6. To recognize, experience and appreciate cultural differences in nutrition, diet, health, and lifestyle of
    Sicily as compared to the United States.
